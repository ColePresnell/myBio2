Cole Presnell


After graduating with degrees in Philosophy and Government from the University of Texas in Austin, Cole began gaining experience in both the private and public sectors. Prior to his work in the mortgage industry, he has held positions in both the private and public sectors, including work at the Texas House of Representatives and as a trader in the US equities markets. Cole, his wife Jessica, and their son Henry were excited to welcome a new baby, Vivienne, to their family in the summer of 2014.
